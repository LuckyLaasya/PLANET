Basic Info about the Planets

Planet Fact
Mercury This Planet is the first planet closest to the sun and is also the smallest planets in our solor system.
Venus This Planet is the second planet closest to the sun and is often called "Earth's twin" because they both are same in size and structure, but is very hot unlike Earth.
Earth This Planet is the third planet closest to the sun and this is the planet where we all live in, this is our Home Planet.
Mars This Planet is the fourth planet closest to the sun and this planet orbits around the sun for 687 Earth days.
Jupiter This Planet is the fifth planet closest to the sun and this planets orbits around the sun for 12 Earth years.
Saturn This Planet is the sixth planet closest to the sun and has a ring around it that is made of space dust and particles.
Uranus This Planet is the seventh planet closest to the sun and this planets orbits around the sun for 84 Earth years.
Neptune This Planet is the eighth planet closest to the sun and this planets orbits around the sun for 165 years.
